title: Stepping Feet Illusion
arialabel: >-
  Vertical black and white lines. A white rectangle moves across the screen
  under the white vertical lines and on top of the black ones. A black rectangle
  moves across the screen on top of both colored lines.
---


Stepping feet illusion is a very famous psychological experiment
Both the bricks will appear to move at different speed
even though they are moving at the same speed.
Click the mouse inside Canvas to confirm that
they are moving at the same speed.
Contributed by Sagar Arora.
