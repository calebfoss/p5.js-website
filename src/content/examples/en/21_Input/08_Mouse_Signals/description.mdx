---
title: Mouse Signals
arialabel: >-
  Three rows: fuschia on the top and bottom rows and white in the middle row.
  The top row tracks the x-coordinates of the mouse, the middle row tracks the
  y-coordinates, and the bottom row tracks whether or not the mouse is pressed.
---


Move and click the mouse to generate signals.
The top row is the signal from "mouseX", the middle row is
the signal from "mouseY", and the bottom row is the signal
from "mouseIsPressed".
