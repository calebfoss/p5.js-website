---
title: Video Pixels
arialabel: >-
  Video is turned into black circles to look like pixels. Pixel size increases
  as the user’s mouse is dragged to the right and decreases as the user’s mouse
  is dragged to the left
---


Load a video, manipulate its pixels and draw to canvas.
To run this example locally, you will need a running
<a href="https://github.com/processing/p5.js/wiki/Local-server">local server</a>.
